Hifz & Tajweed
Excellence Program

A structured journey for the youth of Arusha to master the word of Allah with proper pronunciation, discipline, and deep understanding.

Our Learning Path

Level 01

Tajweed Basics

Mastering the rules of recitation, articulation points (Makharij), and melodic flow.

  • Basic Pronunciation
  • Makharij Points
Level 02

Juz 'Hifz

Memorizing individual Juz with a focus on individual Surahs and correct application of Tajweed.

  • Surahs
  • Monthly Evaluations
Advanced

Full Hifz

A personalized timeline to complete the memorization of the entire Quran Al-Kareem.

  • Custom Pace
  • Ijazah Preparation
