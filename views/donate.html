Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation secure?

Yes. All donations are processed through secure payment verification channels monitored by the executive desk.

Can I receive a receipt?

Yes. An official digital donation receipt will be issued via your email after verification checks wrap up.

Can I donate monthly?

Yes. You can choose either a one-time execution prompt or a structured recurring monthly payment framework.
